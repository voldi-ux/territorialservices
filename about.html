About Our Company

Territorial Services is a fully integrated janitorial cleaning & security services company that provides comprehensive, high quality, reliable cleaning and security solutions to commercial, corporate, industrial and residential clients.Our diligent management and work ethic are central to our service business philosophy and critical to delivering consistent, quality cleaning & security services. We pride ourselves on making our management accountable to the client through direct access and interaction with our managing director.

Our Commitment

For us “a better performance” comes from combining its promise in terms of quality of service, with its commitment to motivating each employee, whilst maintaining an entrepreneurial approach.

Our Mission

We aim to build long lasting professional relationship’s, ensuring total customer satisfaction. Every step we take to ensure the safety of your staff, operations and your brand’s reputation, is bespoke and tailored to your needs.
